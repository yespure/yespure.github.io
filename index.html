Tiancheng Li
Hey.

My work focuses on creating unique narrative perspectives through various media,

using micro-narratives to evoke overlooked emotions and deeply explore seemingly ordinary issues.

Media is a bridge, an interface for the collision of perspectives between people and for mutual understanding.

I hope to use various methods to encourage people to examine themselves and,

in a peculiar way,

bring back the concepts and emotions they have overlooked.

“Souls bound by gravity.” — Char Aznable
